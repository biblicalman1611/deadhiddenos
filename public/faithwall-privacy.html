Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

The short version: FaithWall runs entirely on your device. No browsing data is ever sent anywhere. We don't know what you block, what you visit, or when you use it. There is nothing to sell or share because there is nothing to collect.

How FaithWall Works

FaithWall uses your browser's built-in content blocking system (Chrome's declarativeNetRequest API on desktop; equivalent APIs on iOS). The blocklists are bundled directly into the extension and app — they live on your device, not on a server we control.

When you visit a site, your browser checks the local blocklist and blocks or allows the request. That decision happens entirely on your device. No request information, no domain lookups, no traffic patterns leave your device.

What We Collect

From the extension and iOS app: nothing. No analytics, no crash reports, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs embedded in the extension or app.

From this website (faithwall.deadhidden.org): if you sign up for founding access, we store your email address. That's it.

Email Signups

If you submit your email address through our founding access waitlist, we store it in our database and use it solely to communicate about FaithWall — release updates, access notifications, and occasional product news. We don't sell it, rent it, or share it with any third party.

To be removed, email adam@deadhidden.org with your address and we'll delete it promptly.

Children's Privacy

FaithWall is designed for households, including households with children. We do not collect any personal data from minors. The extension and app themselves collect no data at all — the only data path is the optional email signup on this website, which is intended for adult household heads setting up protection for their family.

Third-Party Services

The extension and app contain no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels. There are no calls to Google Analytics, Firebase, Meta Pixel, or similar services within the extension.

This website (faithwall.deadhidden.org) may include standard web infrastructure. We don't run advertising on it.

Data Retention

Email addresses submitted for founding access are retained until you request deletion. We don't retain browsing data because we never collect it.

Contact

Any privacy question, data deletion request, or concern: adam@deadhidden.org. We respond within a few business days.
