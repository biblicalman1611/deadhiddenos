FAITHWALL PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: May 7, 2026

THE SHORT VERSION
FaithWall runs entirely on your device. No browsing data is ever sent anywhere.
We don't know what you block, what you visit, or when you use it. There is
nothing to sell or share because there is nothing to collect.

HOW FAITHWALL WORKS
FaithWall uses your browser's built-in content blocking system (Chrome's
declarativeNetRequest API on desktop; equivalent APIs on iOS). The blocklists
are bundled directly into the extension and app — they live on your device,
not on a server we control.

When you visit a site, your browser checks the local blocklist and blocks or
allows the request. That decision happens entirely on your device. No request
information, no domain lookups, no traffic patterns leave your device.

WHAT WE COLLECT
From the extension and iOS app: nothing. No analytics, no crash reports, no
telemetry, no third-party SDKs.

From this website (faithwall.deadhidden.org): if you sign up for founding
access, we store your email address. That's it.

EMAIL SIGNUPS
If you submit your email address through our founding access waitlist, we store
it in our database and use it solely to communicate about FaithWall — release
updates, access notifications, and occasional product news. We don't sell it,
rent it, or share it with any third party.

To be removed, email adam@deadhidden.org with your address.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
FaithWall is designed for households, including households with children. We do
not collect any personal data from minors. The extension and app collect no
data at all. The only data path is the optional email signup on this website,
which is intended for adult household heads.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
The extension and app contain no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or
tracking pixels.

DATA RETENTION
Email addresses submitted for founding access are retained until you request
deletion. We don't retain browsing data because we never collect it.
